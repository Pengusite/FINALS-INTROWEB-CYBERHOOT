Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.

Can I suggest topics for new articles or resources?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions. Please use the "Content Suggestion" subject when contacting us.

Do you offer cybersecurity training for organizations?

Yes! Please select "Partnership Opportunity" as your subject and provide details about your organization's needs.

How can I report a security vulnerability on your website?

Please email us directly at security@cyberhoot.com with details about the vulnerability. We take security seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure.
