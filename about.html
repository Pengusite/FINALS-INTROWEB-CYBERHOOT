Why Cybersecurity Awareness Matters

In today's interconnected world, cyber threats are evolving at an unprecedented pace. From phishing attacks to ransomware, data breaches to identity theft, the risks are real and growing. However, many of these threats can be prevented or mitigated with proper awareness and best practices.

Key Statistics:

  • Over 90% of cyber attacks begin with a phishing email
  • The average cost of a data breach is over $4 million
  • A new cyber attack occurs every 39 seconds
  • Small businesses are targets of 43% of cyber attacks

These statistics highlight the critical importance of cybersecurity awareness. At CyberHoot, we're committed to reducing these numbers by educating and empowering our users.
